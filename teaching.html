Teaching Statement

My teaching philosophy emphasizes student engagement, critical thinking, and active participation. I strive to create an inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued and challenged.

Engagement

I believe in the power of interactive teaching methods to spark curiosity and keep students actively involved in the learning process.

Critical Thinking

Promoting problem-solving skills by encouraging students to analyze, evaluate, and create.

Inclusivity

Encouraging teamwork and mutual support to build a strong and inclusive community.
